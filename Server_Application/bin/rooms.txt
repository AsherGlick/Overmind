Officeroom Database
office rooms are stored in this fashion:
Name Start_time End_time MAC
where:
Name is the unique id of the office room
Start_time is the time that the room is open and available to reserve, presented in standard military time
End_time is the time that the room is closed, represented in standard military time
MAC is the mac address of the machine in the officeroom
